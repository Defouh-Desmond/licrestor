Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to some of the most common questions about LIC Restaurant.

How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation directly on our website through the reservation page or by calling us at +237 699 123 456. Walk-ins are also welcome!

Do you offer catering services for events?

Yes! We offer catering for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. Contact us for a customized menu and pricing based on your needs.

Do you provide delivery or takeout options?

Absolutely! We offer delivery and takeout services within Douala. Simply order via phone or our online delivery partners.

Can I host private events at the restaurant?

Yes, our restaurant has a private area available for small gatherings, business meetings, or family celebrations. Contact us to book your event.
