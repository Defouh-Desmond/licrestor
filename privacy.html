Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as your name, email, phone number, and address when you register, make a reservation, or participate in our loyalty program.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to process orders, manage your account, send promotional offers, and improve our customer experience.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Communication Preferences

You can choose to opt-out of promotional messages anytime by clicking the unsubscribe link or contacting us directly.

5. Sharing Information

LIC Restaurant does not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data only with trusted partners to provide essential services (e.g., delivery or payment processing).

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
