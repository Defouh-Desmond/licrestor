Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about reservations, rewards, and more.

How do I make a reservation?

You can book your table easily through our online reservation form or by calling our restaurant directly. We recommend booking in advance for weekends.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We offer catering for birthdays, corporate events, and private parties. Contact us for a personalized menu and quote.

Do you provide delivery?

Absolutely! We deliver across Douala through our trusted partners. Simply order online or call to arrange delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money (MTN, Orange), and all major credit/debit cards at our restaurant.

What are your opening hours?

We’re open Monday to Friday from 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM, and weekends from 12:00 PM – 11:00 PM.

Didn’t find your answer?

We’re happy to help! Reach out to our team and we’ll get back to you shortly.
